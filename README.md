📌 Project Overview

This project is part of the Digital Literacy course at VIT Bhopal University.
As a Student Digital Ambassador, the goal is to build a Digital Literacy Portfolio that helps students understand how to effectively and safely use digital tools and platforms.
The project is divided into five tasks, each focusing on a different aspect of digital literacy such as online presence, communication, collaboration, and cybersecurity.

📖 Key Learnings

•	Importance of maintaining a professional digital identity 
•	Safe and responsible internet usage 
•	Effective online communication 
•	Awareness of cyber threats and prevention methods
